## 2. What I solve

Business owners at 20–100 person companies share the same problems:

1. **Disconnected systems** — ERP doesn't talk to CRM, CRM doesn't talk to email, email doesn't talk to documents. Staff manually copy-paste data between tools.
2. **Manual workflows that don't scale** — As the company grows from 20 to 50 to 100 people, manual processes that worked at 10 break. Email responses are slow, orders fall through cracks, onboarding takes too long.
3. **No technical leadership** — The company has grown past the point where the owner or an IT generalist can manage technology decisions. They need someone who can set direction.
4. **Can't capture AI opportunity** — They see competitors using AI but don't know where to start. GCP offers Vertex AI, Gemini, and Document AI — but they need someone who has actually built production systems with these tools.
5. **Scattered infrastructure** — A mix of legacy systems, SaaS tools, and manual spreadsheets with no unified platform or strategy.

**My pitch:** "I connect your business systems to Google Cloud — so your operations run faster, your team works on what matters, and your technology grows with your company."
